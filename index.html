So what's the issue?

The problem with laser particle counters is that it measures a size but Particulate Matter is measured in weight in a given space (µg/m³)

Problem demonstration

What should be your targets?

Targets

WHO recommendations for PM2.5 are:

  • 10 µg/m³ annual mean
  • 25 µg/m³ 24-hour mean

Targets

EPA "Good" level for PM2.5 is:

0 to 15.4 µg/m³ 24-hour mean

Targets

MEP "Good" level for PM2.5 is:

0 to 35 µg/m³ 24-hour mean
